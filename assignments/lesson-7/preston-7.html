Adventure in Preston

SNOWMOBILING! The Preston Hill Climb occurs every year during the Last Weekend in January. It is exciting and spectators can see some of the best snowmobilers in the country.

RECREATION! Awaiting the fishermen and water-skiers who come from miles around are nine reservoirs. Peaceful campgrounds and refreshing hikes are within an easy distance. Snowmobiling is a favorite sport in the Bear River Range of the Wasatch Mountains and the Wellsville and Malad Ranges surrounding our town.
The local hunting opportunities include: deer, elk, moose, pheasant, duck, geese and mountain lion. Hunting and fishing regulations and licenses are available at many of our stores.
